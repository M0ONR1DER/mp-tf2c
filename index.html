About us

  • Server shutdown Q&A

    When are you shutting down the servers?

    For all main servers, Right Now. This includes our legacy TF2C server.

    Will this Discord shut down too?

    No, there are no plans to shut down the Discord anytime soon. The discord server will stay up and be made into a more Generalized Gaming Community.

    Are you leaving TF2 Classified for good?

    No. Despite my complaints about hosting on it, I still find it a very fun game to play. Also I still have plenty of friends who play on it, so why would I leave?

    Will you be hosting servers again in the future?

    Honestly, I don't know. I could do it again in the future when TF2C community atmosphere changes, but I think I'm kinda done with this hobby for now.

    Hey, I paid money for Pub Premium! Am I getting anything back?

    Even though I said that you cannot refund Pub Premium, I still plan on giving out something small as a thank you to everyone who participated as a donor. And if I do decide to host again, your existing perks will not go away.

    What will happen to all the maps and server plugins you hosted?

    Don't worry, I am not touching anything. All server assets and maps are going to stay where they are until they are needed again.

    What will happen to Shitpost Sunday?

    The 50th and final Shitpost Sunday will be on March 8th 2026. It will be passworded so only members of the discord can access it.

    What will happen to this website?

    It's not going away. I am planning to keep it up and change it into my personal website where you can still access this discord.

    Hey, I made/helped with [INSERT THING HERE] on Moon's Pub. Was my work wasted?

    Absolutely Not. Your efforts in regards to Moon's Pub will not be deleted or wasted away. If you still want to make maps/plugins/whatever for us even after we're done, I'm all for it!

    Established on March 4th 2025, Moon's Pub was a TF2 Classified server network that is hosted in the Midwest USA. Since we started, our goal was to create a safe, fun, and unique space for everyone with fair moderation.

    If you have any questions about us, or just want a safe and accepting community of gamers to hang out with, join our Discord. The discord server will remain up after the shutdown of our TF2 Classified servers.
