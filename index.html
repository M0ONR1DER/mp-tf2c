About us

  • Established in March 4th 2025, Moon's Pub has been a TF2 Classified server network that is hosted in the Midwest USA.

    Since we started, our goal was to create a safe, fun, and unique space for everyone. We are dedicated in curating a space for all and to have fair moderation. If you have any questions about us, join our Discord.
