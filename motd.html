MOTD

Welcome to Moon's Pub, Please enjoy your stay and have fun! If you haven't already, check out the homepage for more information on this server after you're done reading this page.

Rules:

  1. No cheating or exploiting, This includes all cheats and all abusive TF2 exploits that give you an advantage.
  2. No Racism or Bigotry, even without context. Punishment is dependent on moderator discretion.
  3. No political talk, period. Includes Extremism, Hate groups, Hate Speech and Pro/Anti Zionism.
  4. Please do not post Sexually explicit content, including topics, innuendos, generally horny content, etc.
  5. Do not tell players to leave. This is a public server. Dependent on moderator discretion.
  6. No giving birth in VC. Music spamming must be played in Proximity Chat. (tf2c_proximity_voice 1)
  7. No Chat Spamming. Includes begging for server perks or admin positions.
  8. Do not abuse !calladmin. Every report is logged, if report was purposely false you will be banned.
  9. No Admin Begging/Harassing. Don't say someone is cheating if you know their not.
  10. No Admin Impersonation. Do not say that you work for this server or you host this server.
  11. Do not claim that this community is doing things it's not. You will be banned.
  12. Do not advertise, period.
  13. No Griefing. Includes Blocking sniper views or Teleporter traps.
  14. Do not directly disrespect admins or other players. Stay Respectful to others and have good sportsmanship.
  15. You cannot abuse server perks to purposely gain a gameplay advantage over others.
  16. Do not disrespect/shittalk other TF2C communities or glorify this community over others.

If it's not listed, it does not mean it's not punishable.

Think you were wrongly banned? You can click here to request a ban appeal. Cheating bans are non-negotiable and cannot be appealed.

Standard Commands:

  • !rules/!help/!commands - Go to this website/MOTD.
  • !calladmin - Call an admin from the discord. Please remember rule 8 when using this command
  • !survey - Show our 2 question survey before you leave.
  • !mapsurvey - Submit a map to be added to Custom Map Rotation or Shitpost Sunday.
  • !removemap - Submit a veto to remove a map from any Custom Server.
  • !discord - Join our Discord Server.
  • !nominate - Nominate a Map for voting.
  • !rtv - Rock the Vote (vote to change map asap)
  • !tp/!fp - Toggle Thirdperson/Firstperson.
  • !critvote - Vote to enable Random Crits for this map.
  • !votescramble - Vote to scramble unbalanced teams.
  • !ignore Username or @all - Mute a player's Text and Voice chat.
  • !unignore Username or @all - Unmute a player's Text and Voice chat.
  • !getvip - Shows how to get VIP Access.
  • !adminsignup - Join our Admin Team.

Donor Commands:

  • !vip - Opens the VIP Menu - Gives you easy access to every perk you can use.
  • !voice 50-150 - Changes your voice pitch, use the standalone command to reset it.
  • !trail/!trails - Gives you a Colored Trail.
  • !civ - Removes your weapons and Enables A-Posing.
  • !wepcolor red green blue - Changes the Color of your Weapons using an RGB value.
  • !pets - Access SourcePets and have a fake npc follow you.
  • !eyerockets - Toggle Monoculus Rockets.
  • !buildinghats - Turn Building Hats On/Off.
